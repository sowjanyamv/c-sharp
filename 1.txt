1.Which are the products supplied by Specialty Biscuits, Ltd. and the category Name.
SELECT P.ProductName,C.CategoryName
FROM Categories C,Products P,Suppliers S
WHERE C.CategoryID=P.CategoryID
AND P.SupplierID=S.SupplierID
AND SupplierName='Specialty Biscuits, Ltd.';

2.How many products are sold by each suppliers
SELECT S.SupplierID,COUNT(ProductID) AS 'TOTAL_PCOUNT'
FROM [Products] P,[Suppliers] S
WHERE P.SupplierID=S.SupplierID
GROUP BY S.SupplierID;
